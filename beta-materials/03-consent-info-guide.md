# 3. Participation Guide for Families / 家庭参与指南

**EN — Getting started in 4 steps**

1. **Sign up & consent.** Complete the sign-up form and the consent form.
2. **Open Numi.** Use the link/access we send. Start with the welcome screen and the built-in walkthrough.
3. **Play a little, often.** Aim for ~10 minutes a day. Let your child explore the three worlds at their own pace. It's okay to repeat games.
4. **Share feedback.** When we send a short survey, tell us what your child loved, what confused them, and anything that broke.

**Tips for a good experience**
- Sit nearby for the first session or two.
- Praise effort, not just correct answers.
- Keep sessions short and positive — stop while it's still fun.
- Note anything surprising (a favorite game, a tricky moment) to mention in feedback.

**Need help?** Email [email] — we usually reply within [X] hours.

**中文 —— 四步快速上手**

1. **报名并签署同意书。** 填写报名表和家长同意书。
2. **打开 Numi。** 使用我们发送的链接/权限，从欢迎页和内置的操作演示开始。
3. **少量、频繁地玩。** 每天约 10 分钟，让孩子按自己的节奏探索三个世界，重复玩游戏也没关系。
4. **分享反馈。** 收到简短问卷时，告诉我们孩子喜欢什么、哪里感到困惑、有没有出现故障。

**获得良好体验的小建议**
- 前一两次使用时陪在孩子身边。
- 表扬努力，而不只是答对。
- 每次时间短一些、氛围积极——在孩子still觉得有趣时停下。
- 记下任何有意思的发现（最喜欢的游戏、卡住的瞬间），方便反馈时提及。

**需要帮助？** 发邮件至 [邮箱]，我们通常会在 [X] 小时内回复。

# 4. App Walkthrough Document / 应用操作演示文档

A screen-by-screen guide for new users. Pair each step with a screenshot when sharing.
面向新用户的逐屏指南。分享时建议每一步配一张截图。

**EN**

1. **Welcome screen** — Tap **Start Exploring** to begin. Use the top-right toggle to switch between English and 中文.
2. **Walkthrough** — A few intro cards explain the worlds, stars, and the growing face. Tap **Next**, or **Skip**.
3. **Home** — Your hub. From here you can open: your pet, **Explore Numbers**, **Progress**, **Number Sense Check**, and **Join the Beta**.
4. **Explore Numbers** — Choose one of three worlds, or open the **Curriculum Map** to see how games map to skills.
   - World 1 — See Numbers: Quick Count, More or Less, Number Match.
   - World 2 — Where Numbers Live: Number Line, Tens & Ones (place value).
   - World 3 — Number Magic: Make Ten (part-whole). (Add/subtract: coming soon.)
5. **Playing a game** — Each game opens with a simple "How to play" card. Answer the prompts; correct answers advance automatically and fill the progress bar.
6. **Progress & rewards** — See points, badges, and your face buddy. Finishing a world gives the face a new color and unlocks more expressions.
7. **Number Sense Check** — A short self-assessment across key skills.
8. **Settings** — Switch language, revisit **How It Works**, open **Parent Mode**, or view **For Educators**.
9. **Parent Center** — (via Parent Mode) Track learning time, completion, skill progress, and the monthly report.

**中文**

1. **欢迎页** —— 点击 **开始探索** 进入。用右上角切换中英文。
2. **操作演示** —— 几张介绍卡说明世界、星星和会成长的表情。点击 **下一步** 或 **跳过**。
3. **首页** —— 你的主页。可进入：宠物、**探索数字**、**进度**、**数感小测验**、**加入 Beta 测试**。
4. **探索数字** —— 选择三个世界之一，或打开 **课程地图** 查看游戏与技能的对应关系。
   - 世界 1 —— 看见数量：快速数数、比多少、数字配对。
   - 世界 2 —— 数字的家：数轴、十和一（位值）。
   - 世界 3 —— 数字魔法：凑十拆分。（加减法：即将推出。）
5. **玩游戏** —— 每个游戏都以简单的"怎么玩"卡片开始。回答题目，答对会自动进入下一题并填满进度条。
6. **进度与奖励** —— 查看积分、徽章和表情伙伴。完成一个世界，表情会换新颜色并解锁更多表情。
7. **数感小测验** —— 覆盖关键技能的简短自评。
8. **设置** —— 切换语言、重看 **使用说明**、进入 **家长模式**、或查看 **教育者专区**。
9. **家长中心** ——（通过家长模式）追踪学习时长、完成度、技能进展和月度报告。
