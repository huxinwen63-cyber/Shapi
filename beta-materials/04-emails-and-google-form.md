# Part 2 — Google Form Question List / Google 表单题目清单

Build a Google Form with these questions (mirrors the in-app beta sign-up).
Form title: **Numi Beta Tester Sign-Up / Numi Beta 测试报名**
Description: *Help your child fall in love with numbers. / 帮孩子爱上数字。*

用以下题目搭建 Google 表单（与 app 内报名表单一致）。
表单标题：**Numi Beta Tester Sign-Up / Numi Beta 测试报名**
表单说明：*Help your child fall in love with numbers. / 帮孩子爱上数字。*

| # | Question (EN) | 题目（中文） | Type / 类型 | Required |
|---|---------------|--------------|-------------|----------|
| 1 | Your name | 你的姓名 | Short answer / 简答 | Yes |
| 2 | Email | 电子邮箱 | Short answer (validate: email) / 简答（验证邮箱） | Yes |
| 3 | Child's age | 孩子年龄 | Short answer / 简答 | No |
| 4 | I am a... | 你的身份 | Multiple choice: Parent / Teacher / Learning Specialist / School or Organization / Other · 单选：家长/老师/学习专家/机构或学校/其他 | Yes |
| 5 | Main device | 常用设备 | Multiple choice: Phone / Tablet / Computer / Any · 单选：手机/平板/电脑/都可以 | No |
| 6 | Child's preferred language | 孩子偏好的语言 | Multiple choice: English / 中文 / Both · 单选：英文/中文/都可以 | No |
| 7 | How did you hear about us? | 你从哪里听说我们的？ | Short answer / 简答 | No |
| 8 | Anything you'd like us to know? | 还有什么想告诉我们的？ | Paragraph / 段落 | No |
| 9 | I agree to join the beta and receive related emails. | 我愿意参与测试并接收相关邮件。 | Checkbox (required) / 勾选框（必填） | Yes |
| 10 | I am the parent/guardian and consent to participation. | 我是家长/监护人并同意参与。 | Checkbox (required) / 勾选框（必填） | Yes |

**Google Form setup tips / 设置提示**
- Turn on "Collect email addresses" only if you also explain why (privacy).
- Add a confirmation message: *"Thanks! We'll email your invite soon." / "谢谢！我们会尽快邮件发送邀请。"*
- Link responses to a Google Sheet to track sign-ups.
- Consider a separate "Consent" section so the two consent checkboxes feel deliberate.
- 仅在说明用途（隐私）的前提下开启"收集邮箱地址"。
- 添加确认信息：*"谢谢！我们会尽快邮件发送邀请。"*
- 将回复关联到 Google 表格，便于追踪报名。
- 可单独设一个"同意"分区，让两个同意勾选更郑重。

> Tip: The in-app sign-up form (Home → "Join the Beta") collects the same fields, so you can use
> either the Google Form or the in-app form depending on the channel.
> 提示：app 内的报名表单（首页 → "加入 Beta 测试"）收集相同字段，你可以根据渠道选择使用
> Google 表单或应用内表单。
